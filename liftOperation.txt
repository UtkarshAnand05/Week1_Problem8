Details for lift operation for a five floor building :-

Asset :-
No. of floors in the building = 5
Floor No. = Ground, First, Second, Third, Fourth and Fifth

Operation :-
1. Go to a desired floor.
2. Call lift to a desired floor.

States :-
1. Idle - Wait for the user input.
2. Running - Go to a desired floor location.
3. Fault - Go to ground
4. End - Operation terminated
